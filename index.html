PRIVACY POLICY

One Label

NFC Digital Profile Platform • Smart Labels • QR Profiles

Effective Date: 8 May 2026

One Label respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the One Label mobile application, website, smart NFC cards, QR profiles, smart labels, and related services.

By using One Label, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name
  • Designation or profession
  • Profile photo
  • Social media links
  • Website links
  • Business address

b. Profile and Smart Card Information

One Label allows users to create digital profiles shared through NFC cards, QR codes, smart labels, and profile links.

c. Camera Access

Camera access is used only for:

  • Scanning QR codes
  • Scanning smart labels
  • Capturing profile-related images

d. NFC Access

NFC access is used to:

  • Read NFC cards and smart labels
  • Write supported NFC tags
  • Activate NFC products

e. Device and Usage Information

One Label may automatically collect limited technical information for security, diagnostics, analytics, and app performance improvements.

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • IP address and approximate region

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Contact list data
  • Microphone recordings
  • SMS data
  • Call logs
  • Sensitive financial information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Display digital profiles
  • Enable NFC and QR sharing
  • Manage leads and enquiries
  • Improve app security and performance
  • Provide support and updates

4. Public Profile Visibility

Information added to your public profile may be visible to users who scan or tap your NFC card, QR code, or smart label.

5. Third-Party Services

One Label may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Firebase
  • Google Services
  • Cloud Hosting Providers

6. Payments

Payments, if enabled, are processed securely through third-party payment providers. One Label does not store complete payment credentials.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Limited information may be shared only when required for app operations, legal compliance, or service functionality.

8. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to help protect user information, platform integrity, authentication systems, and NFC profile infrastructure.

Security protections may include:

  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Encrypted communication protocols
  • Access control systems
  • Authentication and session management
  • Monitoring for abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access

While we strive to protect user information, no online platform or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

9. Data Retention

User information may be retained for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide One Label services
  • Maintain account functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce platform policies
  • Prevent fraud or abuse

When data is no longer required, reasonable efforts are made to securely delete or anonymize the information.

10. Account and Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their One Label account and associated data at any time by contacting:

tid.advertisr.marketers@gmail.com
